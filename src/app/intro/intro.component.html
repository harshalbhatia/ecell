What is Dr. AIT Mega Summit?

Rotaract Club, in association with the Entrepreneurship Cell of Dr Ambedkar Institute of Technology, present a way for future entrepreneurs to get involved.

We work towards bringing awareness about various renowned startups and share their success stories. We aim to motivate and guide the brightest and most creative minds to bring about their innovate ideas, so that they can become the successful entrepreneurs of tomorrow. The event is filled with wonderful workshops, extravagant events and stupendous speakers to celebrate the spirit of entrepreneurship.

Men are mortal. So are ideas. An idea needs propagation as much as a plant needs watering. Otherwise both will wither and die.
